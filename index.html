Terraced Fields in Sapa

The mountaint town of Sapa is the perfect base for discovering the wonders northern Vietnam. Just a short hike or drive from the town, you'll find incredible valleys, with rice terraces blanketing the slopes from misty peaks down to rushing streams. Sapa has trails for every kind of trekker, and you can plan multi-day hikes to spend more time amid these amazing landscapes.
